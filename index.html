Mrinmoy Bhattacharjee (Ph.D.)

I am currently engaged as a Senior Project Engineer in a MeitY, Govt. of India sponsored project at Indian Institute of Technology Dharwad in Karnataka, India. I pursued my Ph.D. in Signal Processing at Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati in Assam, India. I have submitted my Ph.D. thesis am waiting for my final defense seminar. Previously, I completed my M.Tech. in Computer Technology from National Institute of Technology Raipur in Chattissgarh, India. I completed my B.Tech. in Information Technology from Assam University Silchar in Assam, India. I always strive to develop my skills and qualities. I look forward to challenging professional experiences.

The title of my Ph.D. thesis is "Analysis of Speech and Music Content for Movie Genre Classification". For my Ph.D. work, I tried to perform movie genre classification using only the audio modality of movie trailers. I studied the speech and music signals in general and in the context of movies and TV broadcast signals and developed methods for their efficient discrimination. Subsequently, I used the speech and music information extracted from movie audio using the proposed algorithms for the genre classification of movies.

I have recently joined IIT Dharwad as a Senior Project Engineer in a project under the National Language Translation Mission of MeitY, Govt. of India. The project aims to develop efficient speaker recognition tools for different Indian languages. I am developing an open-source python-based toolkit to build speaker recognition systems for Indian languages. Before joining the Ph.D. program, I worked as an Assistant Project Engineer in a DST, Govt. of India sponsored project titled "Online handwriting recognition for Assamese language" at IIT Guwahati.
